The Avalon Experience
Live Modern. Live Up.

Make Yourself at Home

We believe elevating where you live is about blending it seamlessly with how you live. At Avalon, we go to great lengths designing amenities and choosing locations that put everything within reach. Where you live, is where you come alive. Steps from the TD Garden, in an area flourishing with convenience, entertainment, and possibility, Avalon North Station offers a new standard in apartment living, delivering unparalleled city views and a unique balance of urban living with a neighborhood feel. Our smoke-free, pet friendly residences offer open floor plans in a variety of studio, one-, two-, and three-bedroom layouts, including Signature Collection penthouse homes. And now with our Furnished+ program, apartment living is easier than ever before. Appreciate the stylish furnishings and enjoy the convenience of pre-installed Wi-Fi and cable TV, flexible lease-terms, and utilities that are included in your rent. With Furnished+, you can step right into the life you want to live, without all the hassle. Upgrade any home to Furnished+ and enjoy the convenience of turnkey living. Plus, our technology package, AvalonConnect, offers pre-installed high-speed and community-wide WiFi, and smart technology and services including the ability to submit maintenance requests and pay your rent online, and more. Live your life effortlessly with amenities that include a state-of-the-art fitness center, rooftop lounge and terrace with views of the downtown skyline and Boston Harbor, and garage parking. Located near I-93, Avalon North Station provides easy access to all of Boston, Cambridge and beyond via North Station, including a covered arcade to the MBTA Commuter Rail, Orange Line T, Green Line T, and Amtrak. This is not just apartment living. This is living up.
